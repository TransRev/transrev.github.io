About TransRev

Aims at interconnecting the common citizen with the public transportation system by providing them with simple yet attractive facilities that will encourage them to use the public transport on a more regular basis.

Benefits

The main focus in implementing our solutions is to build a consistent public transportation system which improves the passengers ridership and experience.

Real time transit tracking system for Vehicles

The focus and goal of our system is to provide passengers with an reliable transportation experience to increase ridership. It gives entities access to a platform where they will have access to view in real-time the location of the public transportation vehicles (buses), routes and their estimated time to reach the user preferred location.
